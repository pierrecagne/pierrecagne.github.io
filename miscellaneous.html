Stacks in groupoids as homotopy limits

Preparatory notes (in english) for a talk given in the course "Moduli problems, stacks and simplicial presheaves" by Gabriele Vezzosi. Based on the work of Sharon Hollander, I detail how the condition on a category fibered in groupoids to be a stack can be reformulated as a descent condition.
